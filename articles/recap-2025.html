January was mostly a continuation of 2024. I worked on:

I wasn't comfortable with the idea of being in a class where technical exchanges were limited to a handful of people. We held a few sessions over two months...

I made an effort to stay in public a lot, talk to new people, smile and laugh more often. I also bought myself a new pair of glasses because in the meantime, I had to zoom to 185% to read on my computer.

In short! January went well :)

July 2025

Summer vacation!

We continue with the internship, I'm starting to see things more clearly ðŸ˜‚. And I make a lot of progress on the project during the month.

In parallel, we launch THE LAB with colleagues. The idea is to deconstruct myths, raise awareness and inspire vocations among young people for a better future around artificial intelligence. August 2025

This is the month I went out the most.

I worked on Fluxy, an application that automates the extraction of information from banking documents intended for accounting firms. I worked with a colleague on the project. I was responsible for the software part and he for the business part, but the project ultimately didn't come to fruition. First due to a lack of maturity on both sides, then due to personal constraints.

I had an interview for an AI developer position at a company. Everything went well and I was selected, but I ultimately declined to focus on my studies and personal projects.

October 2025

This month, I focused much more on the Tekbot competition. I alternated between classes and hours in the lab. Each day I learned more and failed a lot.

November 2025

I continue with Tekbot, the specialization courses at school (Optimization, NLP) are very interesting and most importantly, I gave my first official public presentation.

During BWAI (Benin Workshop on Artificial Intelligence), a seminar bringing together researchers, students and AI enthusiasts, I was able to give a presentation on evolutionary algorithms: "Evolutionary Algorithms: Principles, Variants and Applications".

After that, I attended IndabaX Benin.

December 2025

December was a fairly calm month, a month of rest and also a month of gratification. I developed an interest in the world of scientific research and apparently, it shows. My professor then integrated me into the Institute's laboratory. God willing, in the coming months I'll be working with him on themes as interesting as each other.

May God keep us!
